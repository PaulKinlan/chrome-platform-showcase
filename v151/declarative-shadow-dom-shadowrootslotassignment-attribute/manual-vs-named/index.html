Manual vs Named Slots

A live comparison of named-slot (default) and manual-slot (shadowrootslotassignment="manual") assignment in declarative shadow DOM. Click "Assign" to programmatically move children between manual slots — something impossible with named-only assignment.

Named Slots (default)

Children placed via slot="a" / slot="b" attributes. Cannot reorder without changing HTML.

Manual Slots (slotAssignment: manual)

Children assigned via slot.assign(). Click to rotate.
