Minimum Privilege Planner

Chrome 145 splits the Local Network Access permission into loopback and private ranges. Select which local resources your app actually needs and the planner tells you which permissions to request — and which to leave out — so you follow the principle of least privilege.

Minimum privilege: requesting only the permission your feature actually uses makes the permission prompt easier for users to evaluate, reduces the attack surface if your app is compromised, and keeps you compliant with store review policies.
