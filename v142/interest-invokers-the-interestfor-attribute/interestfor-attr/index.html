interestfor

interestfor wires hover/focus on the source into showing the target popover/dialog/details — purely declarative tooltip-style affordances without JS.

The canonical shape of the API:

<button interestfor="info">More info</button>
<div id="info" popover>
  Hover or focus the button above to reveal me.
</div>
