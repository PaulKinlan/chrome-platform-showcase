Dual-source blending

WebGPU dual-source blending — fragment shader can output two colors used together by the blend equation. Required for proper text/sub-pixel rendering pipelines.

The canonical shape of the API:

const device = await adapter.requestDevice({ requiredFeatures: ["dual-source-blending"] });
