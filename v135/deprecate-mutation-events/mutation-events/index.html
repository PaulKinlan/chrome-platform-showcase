Mutation Events

Synchronous Mutation Events are deprecated. They've been jank generators for two decades; MutationObserver is the replacement.

The canonical shape of the API:

// DOMNodeInserted, DOMNodeRemoved, DOMSubtreeModified,
// etc. are deprecated. Use MutationObserver.
