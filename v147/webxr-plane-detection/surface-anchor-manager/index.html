Surface Anchor Manager

In an AR session with plane detection, you attach virtual objects to detected planes by creating XRAnchor instances — the anchor tracks the plane's pose across frames. Click a detected surface in the top-down view to place an anchored object and watch the anchor registry update.

WebXR plane detection requires AR hardware and an active XR session. This demo simulates a top-down view of a room with detected planes and shows how the surface anchoring workflow operates.
