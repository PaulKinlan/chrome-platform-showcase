Header snippet

## Option A — COOP + COEP (blocks popups and non-CORP iframes) Cross-Origin-Opener-Policy: same-origin Cross-Origin-Embedder-Policy: require-corp ## Option B — Document-Isolation-Policy (Chrome 137+) ## Achieves crossOriginIsolated without restricting popups or embedders Document-Isolation-Policy: isolate-and-require-corp
Document-Isolation-Policy lets a page opt in to cross-origin isolation (crossOriginIsolated = true) without needing COOP, which blocks same-origin popups, or COEP, which requires all embedded resources to opt in. It's designed for pages that need SharedArrayBuffer but can't retrofit their entire infrastructure with COOP/COEP headers.
