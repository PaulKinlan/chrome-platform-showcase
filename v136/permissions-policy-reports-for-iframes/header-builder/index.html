Chrome 136 introduces "Potential Permissions Policy violation" reports for iframes. When the parent's Permissions-Policy header conflicts with the iframe's allow attribute, the browser now sends a report to the parent's Report-To endpoint. This demo generates the headers and predicts the violations you'd see.
