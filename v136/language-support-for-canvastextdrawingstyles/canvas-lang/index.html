Canvas lang

CanvasTextDrawingStyles.lang lets the canvas pick locale-correct glyph variants and shaping. Critical for CJK and other locale-sensitive scripts.

The canonical shape of the API:

ctx.lang = "ja-JP";
ctx.fillText("こんにちは", 10, 30);
