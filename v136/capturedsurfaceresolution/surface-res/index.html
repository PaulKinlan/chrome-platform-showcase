Surface Resolution

Sites can read the captured screen's native resolution + scale factor. Lets remote-desktop apps render UI hit-boxes at the right DPR.

The canonical shape of the API:

const settings = track.getCapturedSurfaceResolution();
console.log(settings.width, settings.height, settings.scaleFactor);
