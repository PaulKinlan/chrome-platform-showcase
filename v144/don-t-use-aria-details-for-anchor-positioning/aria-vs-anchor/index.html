ARIA vs Anchor

aria-details is for accessibility relationships, not visual positioning. v144 separates concerns: use anchor= attributes or position-anchor: for layout, aria-details only for assistive tech.

The canonical shape of the API:

<!-- pre-v144: aria-details was wrongly used
     as an anchor reference, conflating semantics. -->
<button popovertarget="info" anchor="trigger">Open</button>
<button id="trigger" aria-details="info">Details</button>
