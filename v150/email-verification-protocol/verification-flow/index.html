Verification Flow

Pretend you're signing up. Click Verify and walk through the three steps of EVP.

step 1

site → browser

navigator.credentials.get({
  identity: {
    providers: [{
      configURL: "https://example.com/.well-known/evp",
      clientId: "site-app",
    }],
  },
});

step 2

browser → mail provider

POST https://mail.example/.well-known/evp/sign
