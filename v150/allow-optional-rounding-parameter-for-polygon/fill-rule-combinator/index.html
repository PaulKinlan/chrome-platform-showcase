Fill Rule Combinator

polygon() accepts a fill-rule keyword (nonzero or evenodd) and a rounding parameter. Chrome 150 lets you combine them: polygon(evenodd round 12px, …). This demo shows every combination across self-intersecting shapes.

Checking polygon(round …) support…
nonzero (default): fills all enclosed regions based on winding direction. A star's center is filled.
evenodd: alternates fill/empty for each nested region. A star's center is hollow.
round <r> (Chrome 150): rounds every vertex by radius r. Stacks with fill-rule: polygon(evenodd round 12px, …)
