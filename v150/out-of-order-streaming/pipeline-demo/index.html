A ReadableStream → TransformStream → WritableStream pipeline. Each chunk passes through a transform (uppercase + prefix). In "old" mode, a slow chunk stalls all subsequent chunks at the transform stage. In "Chrome 150" mode, fast chunks flow through immediately.
