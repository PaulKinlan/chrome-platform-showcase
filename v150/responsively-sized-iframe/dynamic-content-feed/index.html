Dynamic Content Feed

A social feed or news widget that grows as new posts load is the canonical use case for responsive iframe sizing. Left panel: fixed-height iframe that clips content and shows a scrollbar inside the embed. Right panel: responsive iframe that grows with the feed — the parent page scrolls naturally, no scroll-within-scroll UX.

This demo simulates the resize behaviour in-page since allow-responsive-sizing requires same-origin iframes to also send a Supports-Responsive-Sizing: 1 response header (a server-side opt-in). The simulation uses postMessage + ResizeObserver to replicate what the browser would do natively, then shows the timing difference.
