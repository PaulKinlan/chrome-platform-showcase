      why this matters
      
    
      Before v134, Shared Storage worklets had no way to incorporate audience signals from Protected Audience interest groups; aggregation jobs were "blind" to which IGs the user belonged to. With sharedStorage.interestGroups() exposed inside the worklet, you can join IG membership against your shared-storage state and contribute a per-segment count without any of the IG data ever leaving the worklet.
