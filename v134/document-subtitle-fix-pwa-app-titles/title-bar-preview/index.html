document-subtitle: simulated (install as PWA to see real effect)
When installed as a PWA, Chrome 134 builds the window title as: [App name] — [document-subtitle]. Without document-subtitle, it falls back to [App name] — [document.title], which often produces awkward titles like "Gmail — Inbox (47) - Gmail".
