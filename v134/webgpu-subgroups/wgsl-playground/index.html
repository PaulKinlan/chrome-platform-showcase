what each preset does

  • subgroupAdd — every lane votes its local_index, the intrinsic returns the sum across the subgroup. Workgroup of 64 → first 32 lanes sum to (0+1+…+31)=496, next 32 to 1488.
  • subgroupMax — every lane votes (local_index * 7) % 41, intrinsic returns the max within the subgroup. Different per subgroup based on the modulus pattern.
  • subgroupBroadcast — lane 0 of each subgroup pushes local_index * 100 to every lane.
  • subgroupShuffle — lane i reads from lane (i+3) % subgroupSize.
