What actually stops when Chrome freezes a tab?

Energy Saver freeze pauses the entire JS event loop: setTimeout, setInterval, requestAnimationFrame, and microtasks queued from network responses all stop. Four counters run side-by-side — flip the simulated freeze toggle (or hide this tab + wait) and watch every column go cold together.
