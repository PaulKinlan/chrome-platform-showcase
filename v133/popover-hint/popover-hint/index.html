popover=hint

A new popover type for tooltips. Opening a hint does NOT close an open auto popover. Open the menu, then hover the hint trigger - the menu stays open, the hint shows on top.

I am a popover=auto (menu). Showing a hint will NOT close me.
I am popover=hint. I do not steal the auto stack.
I am popover=manual. I close only when you press my close button.
